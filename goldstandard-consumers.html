Do you know what's in your weed?

Cannabis is tested for pesticides, heavy metals, and microbials. But those results are buried in PDFs you never see. Until now.

In 2025, a Michigan lab was shut down for faking $229 million worth of test results. Consumers had no way to know. The Gold Standard exists so you never have to wonder.

What We Check

🧪

Pesticides

Did it pass? Are any results near the action limit? We flag products that barely squeaked by.

🦠

Microbials

Yeast, mold, E. coli, salmonella — the stuff you definitely don't want to smoke.

⚗️

Heavy Metals

Lead, arsenic, cadmium, mercury. Cannabis plants absorb these from soil.

📋

Lab Status

Did it pass on the first try? Was it remediated? Did it require retesting? We show you the full story.

🔍

Transparency

If a product was remediated, were the steps documented? Transparency matters.

Red Flags

⚠️

Remediated Products

The product failed testing and was "fixed" before being retested. You decide if that's OK.

🚨

Near Action Limits

Pesticide levels above 75% of the legal limit. It passed, but barely.

❌

Failed Tests
