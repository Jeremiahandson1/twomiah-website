How It Works

Physical Cards.
Digital Intelligence.

Every card gets a QR code. Every scan creates a record. The whole hobby gets smarter.

01
Shop Lists Inventory

Store owners add cards through the dashboard. Each card gets a unique QR code printed as an insert or sticker — sleeved with the card.

→
02
Collector Scans the Card

Buyer scans the QR with the collector app. Instantly sees full history, current price, past owners, and which shop it came from.

→
03
Ownership Transfers

Buy, trade, or transfer the card. Ownership updates instantly. The card's provenance record grows — every owner, every price, forever.

→
04
Collection Lives in the App

Every card they own lives in their collection. Want list, price alerts, trade offers — the whole hobby in one place.

Card Provenance

Every Card
Has a History.

From the moment a card gets a QR code, its story is recorded. Who stocked it. Who bought it. What it sold for. Who traded it next.

📜
Full Ownership Chain

Every card shows its complete chain of custody — shop, buyers, traders — from first scan to right now.

📈
Price History

See every price a card has sold for. Know if you're getting a fair deal before you buy. Members get full history graphs.

📱
NFC Tap to Trade

Tap phones together in person to transfer a card instantly. No cash, no platform — just tap and it's done.
