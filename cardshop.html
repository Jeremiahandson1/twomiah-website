The Platform

Three Ways Cards
Move.

01
Marketplace

List your card, set your price. Buyers can purchase instantly or send an offer. Every transaction goes through the platform — secure, documented, done.

02
Direct Trade

Card for card. Propose a trade to any collector. Both parties confirm, cards transfer instantly. Build your collection without spending a dollar.

03
Make an Offer

See a card you want that isn't listed? Send an offer. Owner gets a notification and can accept, counter, or pass. The best deals happen off the listing page.

Features

Built for Collectors,
Not Flippers.

📜
Card Provenance

Every card has a history — who pulled it, who owned it, what it sold for. Full transaction log builds trust and adds value to special cards.

📈
Price History

See a card's sale history over time. Know if you're buying at the right price. Know when to sell. Make smarter moves with real data.

⭐
Seller Ratings

Every transaction earns ratings. Build your reputation. High-trust sellers get a verified badge. Bad actors get filtered out early.

🗂️
Collection Manager

Track everything you own in one place. Sort by set, year, player, or value. Know your collection's worth without digging through binders.

🔔
Want List Alerts

Set alerts for cards you're looking for. The moment one gets listed, you're the first to know. Stop refreshing eBay.

💳
Secure Payments

Stripe handles all payments. Sellers link their bank account and get paid directly. No holding funds. No weird workarounds.

Provenance

Every Card
Has a Story.

When you buy a card on Twomiah Card Shop, you know exactly where it's been. Who pulled it. Who owned it. What it sold for. How many times it's changed hands.


This isn't just a neat feature — it changes how cards are valued. A launch-day pull with two owners is worth more than one that's been flipped 14 times. The history is the story, and the story is the value.
