The Problem

$229 million in cannabis products were recalled in Michigan after Viridis Labs was shut down for faking test results. Consumers lost trust. Your clean lab results are sitting in PDFs nobody sees.

Consumers want to know what's in their weed. They can't access lab results at the dispensary. They have no way to compare products by safety. Until now.

The Solution

The Gold Standard is a consumer app where people scan cannabis products and see lab results, contaminant data, and a 0-100 safety score. Think Carfax for cannabis.

When you upload your lab results to our platform, your products become scannable. Consumers see your scores. High scores drive sales. Transparency becomes your competitive advantage.
