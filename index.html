Our Story

Two
Flames.

Carried by a father who wants to leave something for his son.

30 years in sales. That's where this started — not in a computer science class, not at a startup incubator, not with a VC check. In dealerships, in construction, in the real world of small business where the right tool can make or break a year.

Then came the question that changes everything: what if I just built it myself? No background, no roadmap — just the understanding of what was missing and the stubbornness to figure out how to build it.

Twomiah is named for two people — Jeremiah, and Jeremiah Jr. Two flames. One built the foundation. One is going to carry it forward. That's not a metaphor, it's the actual business plan.

Every product we ship is built with that in mind: would we be proud to hand this to someone we love? If yes, it ships. If not, we go back and make it right.
