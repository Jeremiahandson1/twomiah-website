What it does, in 90 seconds.

A real booking system for service businesses — not a generic calendar grafted onto your website.

Knows your crews

Each crew has their own hours, days off, and service zones. The booking page only offers slots a qualifying crew can actually serve.

ZIP code aware

Customer enters their ZIP. We filter to crews that serve that area. Nobody books a job 90 miles outside your service radius.

Two-way calendar sync

Bookings push to Google Calendar and Outlook automatically. When your crew blocks "doctor at 2pm" on their own calendar, that time disappears from your booking page.

SMS confirmations

Customer gets an SMS when they book. Another 24 hours before. Crew gets one the morning of. Powered by your Twilio account (included).

Recurring bookings

"Every other Tuesday, 8 weeks." Customer books once, generates 8 appointments. Cancel the series in one click.

Stripe deposits

Optional deposit per service. Customer pays via Stripe Checkout before the booking confirms. No-shows drop dramatically.

Buffer time

Configure drive time between jobs. The slot generator respects it — no booking for 2pm if you've got something ending at 1:45pm 20 minutes away.

Self-service cancel

Customer can cancel their own booking up to 1 hour before. The slot reopens. Their event clears from your crew's calendar. No phone tag.

Embeddable

Already have a website? Drop a single <script> tag and the booking widget appears as an iframe — even on a non-Twomiah site.
