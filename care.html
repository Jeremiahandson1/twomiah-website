Compliance

Stay Compliant.
Stay Protected.

Home care compliance is complicated — audits, EVV requirements, HIPAA, and state regulations. Twomiah Care keeps you covered so you can focus on care.

  • 🔒
    HIPAA-Ready Infrastructure

    Encrypted data storage and access controls built to meet HIPAA standards for protected health information.

  • 📍
    EVV (Electronic Visit Verification)

    GPS-verified clock-in/out for caregivers, meeting state EVV mandates and Medicaid billing requirements.

  • 📋
    Certification Tracking

    Automatic alerts when caregiver certifications, background checks, or licenses are expiring. Never send an uncertified caregiver.

  • 📁
    Audit-Ready Records

    Complete, timestamped records of every shift, care note, and event — ready to pull for any audit at any time.
